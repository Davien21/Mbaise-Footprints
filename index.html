WELCOME TO MBAISE

Mbaise is an Igbo village in the South-East part of Nigeria, located in Imo State.

It is rich in the Igbo Cultural Heritage and it's citizens are dedicated to adding value to the society.

The mbaise people are a people, with great culture, values and achievements.
